About Us

MANAMAMA active plays is especially made for kid’s lifestyle with family and friends fun activities. We do realize that the most suitable activities for kids involve their family and friends as their time spent together is the key to children’s on-going physical and emotional development.

MANAMAMA active plays is here to provide you activities & sport toys to spend quality time together. Our design are simple and versatile to be use in many ways and adaptive as a child gets older.

Our toys focus on active activities with good benefits, such as

- Tools for physical exercise
- Toys and Games for problem solving
- Understanding Rules & Instructions, Sharing, and Respect
- Building Self-Esteem, Trial & Error, Goal achievement
- Fun, Leisure, and Recreation
- Social interaction

Our product conforms to international safety standards with reliable manufacturing process and high quality materials.

Let your kids grow happy and healthy with you and everyone.
